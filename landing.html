Core Engine

Technical Precision Meets
Fundamental Depth

Every signal is validated by a multi-layer screening engine. No single-indicator guesswork.

150 & 200 SMA Bounce

Detects when price touches the 150 or 200-day moving average within a configurable proximity band. These institutional support levels are where smart money accumulates.

RSI Oversold Confirmation

Requires RSI(14) to drop below your threshold (default 30) before confirming the bounce. Filters out weak pullbacks and catches genuine oversold reversals.

Candle Confirmation

Waits for 2-3 consecutive candles to close above the SMA before flagging the signal. This reduces false positives and confirms directional momentum.

Deep Fundamentals

Revenue growth, profit margins, P/E ratios, debt-to-equity, ROE. Automatically excludes companies with negative revenue growth. Full investment thesis per stock.

WhatsApp Alerts

Multi-Universe Scanning

The Algorithm

4-Layer Signal Validation

Every stock passes through four independent filters. Only the strongest setups survive.

1

Pullback Filter

Stock must be 12-15%+ below its 52-week high. Filters for meaningful corrections, not minor dips.

2

SMA Support

Price is within 2% of the 150 or 200-day SMA. These are the institutional buying zones.

3

RSI + Confirmation

RSI below 30 confirms oversold. Then 3 candles must close above the SMA to prove the bounce.

4

Fundamental Check

Revenue growth must be positive. P/E, margins, and debt are scored to validate the business case.
